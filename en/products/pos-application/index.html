POS application

Comprehensive Electronic Payment and Point-of-Sale Management Services

Service

Generate an invoice to collect payment for goods or services.

Merchants can create a catalog to generate invoices quickly or add new products to create invoices, enhancing the efficiency and accuracy of order processing.

Create a QR code.

To enable customers to pay for goods or services, merchants can generate a QR code. Additionally, merchants can offer multiple payment options, including cash, bank transfers, and credit card payments.

Report

Track sales of goods or services on a daily, weekly, and monthly basis.

Benefits

This facilitates convenient payment for goods and services.

Merchants save time in processing purchase orders.

Enabling faster and more comprehensive service delivery.

Merchants can plan marketing strategies.
