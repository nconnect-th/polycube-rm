Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC)

Currency conversion service through credit card terminals, allowing foreign cardholders to pay for goods or services in their native currency.

Services

Foreign cardholders can choose their preferred payment currency.

Or withdraw cash in their native currency at the exchange rate applicable on the transaction date through credit card terminals, such as Electronic Data Capture (EDC) devices, website payment gateways, or Automatic Teller Machines (ATMs).

The credit card terminal displays information on its screen.

To inform the cardholder, the credit card terminal displays the amount in Thai Baht, the exchange rate on the transaction date, and the equivalent amount in the cardholder’s native currency, allowing foreign cardholders to select their preferred transaction option.

Details of the Sales Slip

The sales slip will display the amount in Thai Baht, the exchange rate on the transaction date, and the amount in the foreign cardholder’s native currency.

Merchants utilizing the currency conversion service will receive the transaction amount transferred in Thai Baht.

Meanwhile, foreign cardholders will know the exact amount to be paid for goods or services in their native currency.

Benefits

Cardholders can choose to pay for goods or services in their native currency.
The cardholder will be informed of the amount payable for goods or services in the cardholder's currency, based on the amount shown on the sales slip as of the transaction date. 4o
The cardholder will not bear the risk of exchange rate fluctuations.
