Frequently Asked Questions

Electronic Data Capture (EDC) Electronic Data Capture (EDC)

What supporting documents are required for merchants interested in applying for this service?

In case of individual registration
1.Copy of commercial registration or business license (registration not less than 6 months)
2.Copy of ID card
3.Copy of any bank account page
4.Special/specific business license (if any)
5.Shop photos, such as shop sign, exterior and interior photos, photos of products or services

In case of juristic person registration
1.Copy of certificate of juristic person registration, copy of objectives (certificate validity not more than 3 months and registration not less than 6 months)
2.Copy of ID card or copy of passport and work permit (in case of foreigners)
3.Copy of any bank account page
4.Special/specific business license (if any)
5.Shop photos, such as shop sign, exterior and interior photos, photos of products or services

How long will it take for merchants to receive approval for the service?

Approval will be received within 7-10 business days, counting from the date the supporting documents are received and complete.

How long will it take for merchants to receive the transfer amount into their account?

Merchants will receive the amount after completing the sales summary transaction within 1-3 business days.

What are the ways merchants can contact for information in case of problems with the transaction?

Merchants can contact the phone number provided on the card reader 24 hours a day, or 02-024-6826 during business hours, Monday-Friday 09.00-18.00, and outside business hours or on holidays 084 670 4589.

Can merchants cancel the service?

Merchants can cancel the service, but must be under the specified conditions.

Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC) Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC)

Which merchants are suitable for using the DCC service?

Merchants with foreign customers who use the service and buy products.

What credit cards are eligible for the DCC service?
The merchant must specify the amount in Thai baht or the cardholder's currency to perform the currency conversion.

The merchant enters the amount in Thai baht for the transaction and the credit card terminal converts the amount to the cardholder's preferred amount.
