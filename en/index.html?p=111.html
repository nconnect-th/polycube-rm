About Us

POLYCUBE

PolyCube Co., Ltd. was registered as a legal entity on April 10, 2006, initially under the name DCC Co., Ltd. The company began its business by pioneering the first website-based currency conversion system (Payment Gateway) in Thailand. This service was offered through the payment gateway of Paysbuy Co., Ltd., which was Thailand’s first online payment service provider at that time.

Due to the significant popularity of its website currency conversion service, the company formalized its corporate structure to support business growth. In 2007, the company expanded its services to include currency conversion for payments made via Electronic Data Capture (EDC) terminals and Payment Gateways for merchants of Siam City Bank (SCIB), which is now TMBThanachart Bank (TTB). In partnership with the bank, the company offered dynamic currency conversion supporting the largest number of currencies available at the time – 16.

Currently, the company provides currency conversion services via credit card terminals supporting a total of 38 currencies.

Subsequently, in 2009, the company partnered with Siam City Bank (now TMBThanachart Bank) to provide currency conversion services for Mastercard credit cards via Automatic Teller Machines (ATMs). This marked the first instance of a bank in Thailand offering currency conversion services through the cash withdrawal channel. Currently, this ATM service supports up to 113 currencies, and currency conversion services were additionally launched for Visa credit cards via this channel in 2019.

Following the introduction of currency conversion services across Electronic Data Capture (EDC) terminals, Payment Gateways (websites), and Automatic Teller Machines (ATMs), the company expanded its operational scope in collaboration with its business partner banks. This expansion included areas such as sales, customer relationship services, and technical support. Leveraging its experience and expertise in the EDC currency conversion business, the company then initiated its first international service expansion in 2016 by partnering with 2C2P Co., Ltd. to provide EDC currency conversion services to merchants of Myanmar Citizens Bank (MCB).

Later, in 2018, the company changed its name to PolyCube Co., Ltd. to align with its business direction and adapt to the changing landscape of financial technology. In 2021, the company received licenses under the supervision of the Bank of Thailand to operate as a Payment Facilitator and to provide Payment Service Provider Support services. Following this, it launched the “GebTang” payment acceptance application, designed for merchants to accept payments for goods or services, which includes a comprehensive point-of-sale (POS) management system.

Drawing upon over 17 years of experience collaborating with partner banks, during which the company has accumulated extensive knowledge and expertise in EDC payment acceptance, sales, customer relationship management, and technical services, PolyCube Co., Ltd. begins offering electronic payment facilitation services via EDC terminals in 2025. This service aims to provide payment solutions to all types of businesses throughout Thailand.

POLYCUBE

Mission

PolyCube is a Thai company committed to providing payment acceptance services, utilizing modern financial technology, to businesses in Thailand. Offering services through both proactive and reactive approaches, PolyCube aims to meet the payment needs of all types of businesses across the country.

Vision

PolyCube is committed to providing comprehensive payment acceptance services that adhere to international security standards. The aim is to elevate the quality of payment acceptance services for businesses in Thailand to be comparable with global standards.
